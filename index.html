Instructions

Welcome to the Rock, Paper, Scissors game!

To play the game:

  1. Enter your first name in the input field and click "Submit."
  2. Click on one of the three buttons (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) to make your choice for each round.
  3. The computer will randomly select its choice.
  4. The results of each round will be displayed, showing whether you won, lost, or it's a draw.
  5. The game consists of five rounds.
  6. After five rounds, the game will declare the overall winner - either you, the computer, or a draw.
  7. You can restart the game at any time by clicking the "Restart" button.

Have fun and may the best player win!
